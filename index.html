About Antigua

Antigua is located in the heart of the Caribbean in the Caribbean Sea surrounded by other beautiful islands like Monstserrat, Guadaloupe and St Kitts. Trying to escape the cold there is no better place than Antigua. The temperatures on the island ranges between the mid-seventies to the mid eighties, with higher tempeartures in the summer and lower temperatures in the winter.The island is about 108 square miles and is 14 miles long and 11 miles wide.

Thing to do on the island

Don't let it's small size discourage you there are many wonderful activities to occupy your time. One of the local pass time that the island has to offer is its many beaches.Legend has it's that Antigua has 365 beaches one for each day of the year.The challenge tourist face isn't finding the beaches but picking which ones to visit. The coastes of Antgua with constant trade winds makes it ideal for Sailing and is the host to the largest charter yatch show in world. This event starts in late December and concludes in Janurary. However this isn't the only sailing activity in antigua.There is also Sailing week there is fundrasing, sporting events and many soical events.
