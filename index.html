Welcome to Antigua!

About Antigua

Antigua is located in the heart of the Caribbean in the Caribbean Sea surrounded by other beautiful islands like Montserrat, Guadaloupe and St.Kitts. If your trying to escape the cold there is no better place than Antigua. The temperatures on the island ranges between the mid-seventies to the mid eighties, with higher tempeartures in the summer and lower temperatures in the winter. The island is about 108 square miles, 14 miles long and 11 miles wide.

Thing to do on the Island

Don't let its small size discourage you there are many wonderful activities to occupy your time especially its beaches. Legend has it that Antigua has 365 beaches one for each day of the year.The challenge visitors face isn't finding the beaches but picking the beaches that suit their taste. The coast of Antgua with constant trade winds makes it ideal for sailing and is the host to the largest Charter Yacht Show in the world. This event starts in late December and concludes in Janurary. However, this isn't the only sailing event in Antigua, there is also Sailing Week. A week long yacht regetta packed with races, sporting events, fundraising and many social events.

Antigua also has many unique cuisine and fruits to offer. Below is a list of just some of the exciting foods you can try while in Antigua;

  • Fungee
  • Bread & Cheese (Bread baked in brick oven)
  • Sugarcake
  • Fried Dumpling aka Johnny cake
  • Goat Water
  • Coconut and Pineapple Tarts (made with our black pineapple)
  • Fruits that you can enjoy on the Island
    • Guava
    • Finger Rose or Lady Fingers

    • Guinep
