About Antigua

Antigua located in the Leeward Island in the Caribbean Sea.Surrounded by other beautiful islands like Monstserrat,Guadaloupe and St Kitts.Trying to escape the cold there is no better place then Antigua.The Temperature on the island normally ranges between the mid-seventies to the mid eighties, with higher tempeartures in the summer and lower temperatures in the winter.The island is about 108 square miles and is 14 miles long and 11 miles wide.Don't let its small discourage you there many wonderful things to occupy your time.
