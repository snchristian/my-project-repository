About Antigua

Antigua is located in the heart of the Caribbean in the Caribbean Sea surrounded by other beautiful islands like Monstserrat, Guadaloupe and St Kitts. Trying to escape the cold there is no better place than Antigua. The temperatures on the island ranges between the mid-seventies to the mid eighties, with higher tempeartures in the summer and lower temperatures in the winter.The island is about 108 square miles and is 14 miles long and 11 miles wide.

Don't let its small size discourage you there many wonderful things to occupy your time. One of the local pass time that the island has to offer is its many beaches.Legend has it that antigua has 365 beaches a beach for ever day doesn't that sound fun.Sailing is another activity that is big in antigua. There is even a whole week dedicated to it, Sailing Week starts near the end of april and bring in many beautiful vessel to take part in the event.
